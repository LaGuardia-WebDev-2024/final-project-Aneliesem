About Me

Hi, I’m Aneliese! I’m a student at LaGuardia High School, majoring in dance. Movement has been a part of me since I was four years old, and I’ve poured my heart into every step since then. I’m passionate about performing, storytelling, and using my body as a language of expression.

Lately, I’ve been drawn toward the fashion world — especially fashion marketing. I love how style tells a story, much like choreography. I’m excited by the idea of combining creative direction with branding, turning bold ideas into something visual and unforgettable. Whether I’m dancing or designing, I care about aesthetics, emotion, and making a statement.
